# Clear Health User Manual

Welcome to Clear Health! This guide will help you get the most out of our intelligent health monitoring platform.

### 1. Account Setup

- **Registration**: Sign up using your email and a secure password.
- **Biometrics**: Upon first login, you'll be prompted to enable Biometric authentication (Fingerprint or Face ID) for quick and secure access.
- **Profile Creation**: Set up your primary health profile by entering your basic information.

### 2. Managing Family Profiles

Clear Health allows you to manage health data for your loved ones.

- Go to **Settings** -> **Profiles**.
- Tap **Add Profile** to create a new record for a family member.
- Switch between profiles easily from the Dashboard to view specific health data.

## ğŸ“Š Using Core Features

### 1. Uploading Lab Reports

- Tap the **"+" (Upload)** button on the home screen or Lab Results page.
- Select a PDF file from your device.
- Wait a few moments while our AI analyzes your report.
- Review the extracted data and save it to your profile.

### 2. Viewing Insights

- Open any saved lab result.
- Tap **"AI Insights"** to see a summary of your results.
- Review the **"Optimization Tips"** for actionable health advice.

### 3. Tracking Trends

- Navigate to the **Trends** tab.
- Select a metric (e.g., "Hemoglobin") to see its history.
- Use the **Compare** tool to select two different dates and see exactly what changed.

### 4. Health Chat

- Tap the **Chat** icon.
- Ask questions like: "Show me my cholesterol levels from last year" or "What do these results mean for my heart health?"
- The AI assistant uses your personal history to provide specific answers.
